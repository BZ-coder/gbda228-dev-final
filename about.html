BACK IN 2008,

It all began with two brothers and a dream. Having recently travelled from Italy, we sought to share their traditional recipes and create a thriving business in Canada.

Things started out slow when we first put up business, building our reputation and notability from the ground up. Soon, the public began to take notice.

With freshly sourced ingredients straight from our homeland, we make sure to give reason for one of our slogans:

“A smile in every slice”

Soon after the boom in business, we began welcoming new staff into
the family. Following that, we began to deliver our pizza so folks
could enjoy pizza in their homes with a click of a button or a call.
We aspire to meet the needs of our highly esteemed, loyal customers.
